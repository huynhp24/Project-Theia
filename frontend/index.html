SERVICES

Scenes and Objects

Identify thousands of objects (such as building, vehicles, pets, furniture), and scenes (such as parking lot, sunset, beach, city)

Text

Detect text in images: text in natural scenes (road signs, license plates), text over objects (t-shirts, mugs), and text on screen (captions, news)

Person

Detect when faces appear in images and get attributes (such as gender, age range, eyes open, glasses, facial hair) for each

Audio

Converts text to lifelike speech to read labels and texts in images

About Us

We are a team of software engineering students who were inspired by cloud computing and want to put our knowledge into practice by assisting individuals with visual impairments to "see" their photographs by hearing detailed explanations. We want people who speak different languages to be able to experience it in various parts of the world. Therefore, our descriptive script can be spoken in a variety of languages. We're happy that we were able to finish the project and bring joy to people who are blind or visually disabled.
